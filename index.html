About us

From 1820 Chateau Roux offers the tranquillity and magical atmosphere of a castle in middle of Normandy.
Own by the Roux family from over two centuries, the Chateau offers an experience of communion with nature and awakening of the senses with the tasting of fine wines and meals prepared with care by the best chefs of France.
Here at Chateau Roux you will find the care of a five-star hotel and the affection and attention of a family who loves his work and performs it with devotion.
Visit us with your family or come by yourself to find relax, peace and an enchanting view.
